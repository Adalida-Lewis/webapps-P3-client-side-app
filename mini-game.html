Play a Game of Fifty-Two

How to play?

Enter a starting number that is between the values of 1 and 10.

Continue to click the add button until you are close to the number 52.

Keep an eye on your number! The add button can generate a number between 1 and 10.

Click the hold button when you are close.

Keep in mind the Bot can also hold and getting a tie does not mean a win.

Good Luck! Have Fun!
